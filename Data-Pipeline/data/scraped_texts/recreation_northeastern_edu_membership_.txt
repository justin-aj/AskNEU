MEMBERSHIP

[PARAGRAPHS]
Recreation membership provides access to all University Recreation facilities (the Marino Recreation Center, The SquashBusters Center, & the Cabot Physical Education Center), and the ability to purchase entry to other fee-based programs, such as group fitness, personal training, instructional program, & intramural sports. Please refer to each group tab, under the main menu, for additional information.

Please use the ‘Membership’  menu to see our different membership types.
